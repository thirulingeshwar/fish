Grilled Salmon

Juicy salmon grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • Fresh dill (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Place the salmon on the grill, skin-side down. Cook for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Flip the salmon and cook for another 3-4 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh dill before serving.
