Fish Curry

Spicy and flavorful fish curry.

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Mix well.
  5. Add coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  6. Gently add the fish fillets and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice.
